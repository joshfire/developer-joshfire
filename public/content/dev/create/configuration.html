Configuration

The configuration page allows you to change the settings of your application. The page has sub-pages on the left: General lets you change the global settings of your application, while Templates and Add-ons sub-pages let you change settings specific to selected templates and add-ons.

Global configuration

On this screen, you can change the title of your application, adding an application icon that will be used at deploy and adding a brand logo that may be used by templates.

Updates

When you see a small red circle near the Configuration tab, it means that a new version of one or more of your installed items (template or add-on) is available in the Factory.

Templates configuration

On the left, you can see the names of every template installed in your application. By clicking on one of this name, you access the template configuration page. Read more about how to configure a template on this page.
