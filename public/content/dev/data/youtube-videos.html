Youtube data source

Description

The Youtube data source returns a list of videos. To play videos, templates should have a video player, it is the case of many templates fromthe Factory.
If you want to publish your own videos, you first have to upload them to Youtube, and thus have a Google account. Only videos published publicly on Youtube can be fetched.

Configuration

First, you have to choose how you want to search your results, available options are:

  • Text
  • Playlist
  • Username

If you want to keep a total control on the video displayed in your application, we encourage you to use the "Playlist" search and to create your own playlist.

Search by Text

Enter in the "Search text" field the keyword you want to search for. The given results will be the results Youtube returns for this keyword, sorted by relevance.

Search by Playlist (recommended)

Enter in the "Playlist URL" field the URL of a Youtube playlist created by you or someone else. A playlist URL looks like this: http://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL3394620DFBD39343

How to create your own playlist on Youtube?

To create a new playlist, go on a video page and then:

  1. Click the "Add To" button under the video you're watching.
  2. From the drop down menu, choose Add new playlist
  3. Type in the name of your Playlist and hit enter.
  4. Add a note and choose if you want your Playlist to be public (viewable by others)

For more information, please go to the Youtube help pages:

  • How do I make a Playlist?
  • How do I organize the videos in my playlist?

Where to find a playlist page on Youtube?

From a user page:

You can find playlist pages by going to a user page, clicking on its videos and then clicking on "Playlists".

From a search:

Do a search using the main Youtube Search bar at the top. Then, filter the results by clicking "Filter" and selecting "Playlists" in the "Result type" section.

Search by Username

Enter a given Youtube username, videos sorted by upload date will be listed. Your youtube username can be found on the top right of the Youtube screen.
